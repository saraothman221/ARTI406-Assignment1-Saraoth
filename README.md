# Mall Customers Segmentation - EDA Project

## 1. Dataset Description
This dataset contains information about **200 customers** of a shopping mall. It is commonly used for customer segmentation and market basket analysis in Machine Learning.

## 2. Features (Columns):
- **CustomerID:** Unique ID assigned to the customer.
- **Gender:** Gender of the customer (Male/Female).
- **Age:** Age of the customer.
- **Annual Income (k$):** Annual income of the customer in thousands of dollars.
- **Spending Score (1-100):** Score assigned by the mall based on customer behavior and spending nature.

## 3. Project Goal
The goal of this assignment is to perform **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** to understand the distributions of customers and the relationships between their income and spending habits.
